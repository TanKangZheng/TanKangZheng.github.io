Freelance Programmer
My headstart in programming was started via the Game Development and Technology course in Nanyang Polytechnic, growing with each project done while in my tenure there. Developing games alongside others is a passion of mine, fueled by my inspirations of major marvels in today's gaming landscape, I hope to one day be able to use this enthusiasm and stand among the great minds that shaped my childhood.

Today's gaming industry is plagued with coporate giants that rather than make games for the market, create tools to dry consumer's wallets for minimal effort, while keeping their own production costs low, leading to poor products again and again. I strongly believe in pushing back against this all too common practice, where games should be a passion again, not a product.

I duly hope that we can return to that golden age, where games were simply meant to be enjoyed, rather than be milked for every last cent.
